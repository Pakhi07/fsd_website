Mussoorie - The Queen of Hills

Kempty Falls

Kempty Falls is one of Mussoorie's most popular attractions, offering a refreshing escape with cascading waterfalls surrounded by nature.

Mall Road

Take a stroll down Mall Road, filled with shops, eateries, and breathtaking views of the Doon Valley below.

Fun Facts About Mussoorie

  • Mussoorie was established by British officers as a hill station in the early 19th century.
  • It has been a popular destination for both Indian and British tourists since the colonial era.
