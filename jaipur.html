Jaipur - The Pink City

Hawa Mahal

Hawa Mahal, also known as the Palace of Winds, is one of Jaipur’s most iconic landmarks. Built in 1799, it has 953 windows that allow royal women to observe street festivals without being seen.

Amer Fort

Amer Fort is a majestic fort located just outside Jaipur. It’s known for its artistic Hindu-style elements and the famous Sheesh Mahal (Mirror Palace). The light and sound show here is a must-see.

Fun Facts About Jaipur

  • Jaipur is known as the Pink City due to the pink-colored buildings in its old city area.
  • It’s part of the Golden Triangle tourist circuit, which includes Delhi and Agra.
  • Jaipur was India’s first planned city, designed according to Vastu Shastra, the ancient Indian architectural principles.
