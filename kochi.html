Fort Kochi - The historic seaside town of Kerala

Chinese Fishing Nets

Fort Kochi's famous Chinese fishing nets, locally known as "Cheenavala," are a historic legacy from trade relations with China during the 14th century.

St. Francis Church

St. Francis Church, one of the oldest European churches in India, is where Vasco da Gama was originally buried.

Fun Facts About Fort Kochi

  • Fort Kochi was a Portuguese, Dutch, and British colony, and its architecture reflects these influences.
  • It was the first European township in India.
