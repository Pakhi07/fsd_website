Shimla - The Capital of Himachal Pradesh

The Ridge

The Ridge is the hub of all cultural activities in Shimla and offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

Jakhoo Temple

Located atop Jakhoo Hill, this temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman is the highest point in Shimla, offering panoramic views.

Fun Facts About Shimla

  • Shimla was the summer capital of British India.
  • The city is famous for its historic railway, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
